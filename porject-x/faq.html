FAQ

Answers to common questions about MERON PRODUCTION and how to book a session.

What type of photography do you offer?

We cover weddings, portraits, events, product shoots, and creative sessions. If you have a special idea, choose “Other” in the booking form and tell us your vision.

How do I book a session?

Open the Booking page and fill out the form with your preferred date and session type. After you submit, we’ll contact you to confirm availability and details.

How long does it take to receive the photos?

Delivery depends on the session. Portraits are usually faster, while weddings/events can take longer due to editing. We will agree on the delivery time when we confirm your booking.

Do you edit the photos?

Yes. We do professional color correction and retouching while keeping a natural look. If you want a special style (cinematic, black & white, etc.), mention it in your message.

Where are you located and do you travel?

We can work on-location depending on the project. Tell us where your shoot will be and we’ll confirm travel options and any extra costs.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on the session type, duration, and location. Use the Booking page to request a quote, or message us on the Contact page.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes. If you need to reschedule, contact us as soon as possible. We’ll do our best to accommodate changes based on availability.
